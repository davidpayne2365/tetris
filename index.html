How to Use the Rubik's Cube

• Swipe on the cube to rotate it and view all sides

• Use the letter buttons (F, B, R, L, U, D) to rotate faces:

F: Front | B: Back | R: Right | L: Left | U: Up | D: Down

• The ' (prime) means counter-clockwise rotation

• X, Y, Z buttons rotate the entire cube on that axis

• Reset returns to solved state

• Scramble randomly mixes the cube
